About Bad Mojo

  • Brian Mack — Guitar and Vocals
  • Rob Mack — Drums and Vocals
  • Matt Briercliffe — Bass
  • Tyler Tretsven — Guitar

Bad Mojo formed in the Twin Cities in 2014, but they've played together in different bands for a decade. Matt, Tyler, and Brian started playing music together in junior high, and from 2004 and 2007, they played some of Minnesota’s finest venues (First Avenue, Fineline, the Taste of Minnesota) with their band Cigarette Muffin.

Between 2007 to 2011, Brian and his older brother Rob formed a few cover bands — Rewind, Inner City Drive, and Rock Candy (with Tyler on guitar) — and from 2011 to 2013, Brian joined Snaggletoof and the Scott Koffman Band as a drummer.

After a break in 2013, Brian, Rob, Tyler, and Matt decided to get together and jam. After a few sessions, they decided to get out and play live again. Now 2014 is here, and Bad Mojo is ready to rock at a venue near YOU!
